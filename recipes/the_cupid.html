The cupid

This recipe is a sweet favorite of mine. It has a brilliant ruby red colour and is the perfect cocktail before a big night out.

Ingredients

  • 30 mL of Gin (if you can find a Turkish delight gin that works best)
  • 15 mL of orange liqeur (Cointreau is best)
  • 15 mL of freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • 10 mL of apricot nectar
  • 5 mL of grenadine
  • 3 drops of bitters
  • Ice
  1. Place cocktail glasses in fridge to cool.
  2. Combine Gin, Cointreau, lemon juice, apricot nectar and grenadine into a cocktail shaker.
  3. Perform a dry shake to mix all liquids.
  4. Remove glasses from fridge.
  5. Add ice to the cocktail shaker and shake hard for 30 seconds
  6. Pour the cocktail out into the glass using a strainer to catch large bits and ice
  7. Garnish with skewered raspberries.Enjoy your Cupid!
