Classic margarita

This recipe is one developed by my family and I. We enjoy drinking margaritas on a hot summers day. It pairs perfectly well with spicy cuisine such as mexican and even indian dishes. The acidity from the lime balances the mild heat from the chilli salt rim. We recommended a good quality tequila for this recipe.

Ingredients

  • 45 mL of reposado tequila
  • 30 mL of orange liqeur (Cointreau is best)
  • 22 ml of freshly squeezed lime juice
  • Tajin chilli seasoning (for the rim)
  • Ice
  1. Place cocktail glasses in fridge to cool.
  2. Combine Tequila, Cointreau and lime juice into a cocktail shaker.
  3. Perform a dry shake to mix all liquids.
  4. Remove glasses from fridge and rim with a good amount of lime from a lime wedge.
  5. Dip the glasses rim into a good amount of Tajin. Best to have Tajin on a plate
  6. Add ice to the cocktail shaker and shake hard for 30 seconds
  7. Pour the margarita out into the glass using a strainer to catch large bits and ice
  8. Garnish with dried/candied lime wheel.Enjoy your margarita!
