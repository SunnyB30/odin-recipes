The Silver Fox

Another simple sweet recipe, this is an easy to drink alternative to a rum and coke. The ginger elevates the spice in the coca cola and a smooth vodka makes this cocktail all too easy to gulp down.

Ingredients

  • 45 mL of Vanilla Vodka
  • 3 drops Angostura bitters
  • Coca Cola soft drink
  • Thin slice of ginger
  • Ice
  1. This one is too easy. Combine Vodka, bitters and Coca Cola into a tall glass filled with ice and stir.
  2. Add ginger as a garnish on top and serve. Enjoy your Silver fox!
